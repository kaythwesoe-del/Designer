What is called a designer?

A designer is a person who plans the form or structure of something before it is made. They create tangible or intangible products, services, or experiences.

What is a designer job?

A designer develops and creates visual ideas. Depending on the type, it could be magazines, interfaces, or public space systems.

An Artist

Design Art

Graphic design is the art and profession of selecting and arranging visual elements to convey a message to an audience.
